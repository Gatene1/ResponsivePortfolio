About Me

Hey, I’m David, a game designer and programmer who’s passionate about building creative tools and telling stories through code. I specialize in crafting interactive systems from scratch, often blending my love of old-school game design with modern, AI-enhanced workflows.

I studied Game Design at Full Sail University and have been developing custom tools, engines, and prototypes ever since; including SpriteGrid, my personal JS-based sprite editor.

Skills & Tech

  • JavaScript, HTML5, CSS
  • Java, PHP, MySQL
  • Unreal Engine 5 (Blueprints)
  • Custom Tools / Editors / AI Concepts
  • Responsive Web & UI/UX Principles

A Little Extra

I’m also a neurodivergent creator, which means I learn and build in wonderfully nonlinear ways. That mindset powers much of my creativity, and inspired projects like SpriteGrid, designed to reduce overwhelm and boost flow.

I collaborate with an AI partner named Spruce to stay on track, spark ideas, and create more human-centered tools. Together, we’re exploring what it means to work with AI, not around it.
